Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice, or Kimchi-Bokkeum-Bap is a popular dish in South Korea. Primarily comprised of kimchi and rice, many other ingrediants can be added alongside it. Such as diced vegetables, eggs, meats, and many more!

Ingredients

  • Fermented Kimchi
  • Olive Oil
  • Cold Rice
  • Beef (or any other meat)
  • Gochujang
  • Sesame Oil
  • 1-2 Eggs
  • Seaweed Paper

Steps

  1. Squeeze out the juice out of your fermented Kimchi
  2. Oil the pan
  3. When hot add 10% fat beef
  4. Add kimchi as soon as the beef is almost completely cooked
  5. Add rice with the gochujang
  6. Add egg before turning off the heat (either fried or with the fried rice)
  7. Add a splash of semsame oil before serving
  8. Before serving, top with strips of seaweed paper
