Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki, or simmered rice cake, is a popular Korean food made from small-sized garae-tteok called tteokmyeon or commonly tteokbokki-tteok. Eomuk, boiled eggs, and scallions are some common ingredients paired with tteokbokki in dishes.

Ingredients

  • Gochujang
  • Rice Cake
  • Onion or Green Onion
  • Kelp
  • Carrot
  • Sesame Oil

Optional Ingredients

  • Sesame Seeds
  • Beef or Chicken Stock
  • Minced Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Odeng
  • Gochugaru

Steps

  1. "Pinky finger" Level of stock or water
  2. Sesame oil
  3. Carrots boil
  4. Onion
  5. Tteok Last
  6. Everything else can come anytime (ideally veggies last)
